The Challenge & Our AI-Powered Solution

This section outlines the real-world problem our project addresses and introduces our mobile application as a transformative solution. We contrast the limitations of traditional diagnosis with the benefits of accessible, AI-driven technology.

The Problem: Diagnostic Hurdles

  • Growing Prevalence: Hair and scalp conditions affect millions globally, creating a significant healthcare burden.
  • Diagnostic Bottleneck: Manual diagnosis is often slow, subjective, and requires specialized expertise which is not always available.
  • Limited Access: Many regions lack sufficient dermatologists, leading to delayed diagnosis and treatment.
  • Need for Accessibility: A critical need exists for a tool that can democratize early detection and empower patients.

The Solution: Accessible AI

  • AI-Powered Detection: An accurate deep learning model to identify common hair and scalp diseases from a simple photo.
  • Severity Scoring: A system to quantify the severity of the detected condition, helping to prioritize cases.
  • Built-in Explainability: Visual heatmaps (Grad-CAM) show *why* the model made a specific prediction, building trust and aiding verification.
  • Mobile First: A user-friendly mobile app for seamless image capture and instant, on-device diagnosis.
